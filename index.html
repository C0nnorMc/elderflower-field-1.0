Welcome to Faeodyn Field

A cut flower farm located on the Mid North Coast in the beautiful Great Lakes Region. Specialising in seasonal fresh blooms with some of the most loved varieties, Faeodyn Field offers a range of beautiful bouquets, individual bunches, bouquet buckets and flowers for any special occasion. Bouquet making workshops are also run throughout the year. We look forward sharing the magic and beauty of flowers with you.

Seasonal Flowers

Faeodyn Field embraces the philosophy of grow locally, grow naturally, grow sustainably, resulting in beautiful fresh season blooms free from chemicals. Each season, old favourites and exciting new varieties will be available, a genuine direct from field to the vase experience.
